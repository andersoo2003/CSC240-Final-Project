What is Brachytherapy?

Brachytherapy is a form of internal radiation that is used to treat a wide variety of cancers. The procedure involves placing a series of radioactive “seeds” close to the intended target (like a cancerous ). Brachytherapy is a popular form of cancer treatment due to its ability to bypass , unlike radiation therapy (another common cancer treatment).

The goal of this website is to provide a simple introduction into one of the worlds most common forms of cancer treatment. For many, nuclear physics is a difficult field to understand, so this website will cover some of the basics.
